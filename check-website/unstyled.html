Get Alerted when your Website Goes Down

In this tutorial, we'll teach you how to use Grafana Cloud Synthetic Monitoring to check on your website, and alert you if it goes down!

Step 1: Create a Synthetic Monitoring Check

We'll first need to create a check to make sure the website is OK

  • Click Testing & Synthetics > Synthetics > Checks in the Nav Bar
  • Click the "Create Check" button that appears.
  • Click the API Endpoint button
  • Choose a Job Name
  • Fill in an endpoint (e.g. https://grafana.com/)
  • Click the Uptiime button to proceed to the next step
  • For simple websites, you can take the defaults. Customize anything needed, then hit the Labels button.
  • Apply any labels if you want any, but none is OK. Hit the Execution button next.
  • Choose your execution locations. "Do it" chooses just America.
  • Review and customize any other settings you like, then hit the Alerting button at the bottom
  • Check the box to alert if at least one execution fails.
  • Click Save

Congratulations! You've just created your first synthetic check!
